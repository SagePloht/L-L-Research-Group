Strength - The amount of force a person can exert.

Agility - The top speed, acceleration, and acrobatic skills of a person. Essentially general movement.

Dexterity - The hand-eye coordination and reaction speed of a person.

Endurance - The amount of stamina or energy a person has available to use.

Constitution - The durability of the various body parts of a person, as well as their resistence to poisonous substances and illnesses.

Intelligence - The ability to form connections and interpet patterns.

Wisdom - The ability to take in information and decide on the best course of action based on that information.

Perception - The ability to take in a scenario and filter through the useful and useless information. Intuition is part of Perception.

Willpower - The strength of a person's mind and will., the control one has on their own mind.

Memory - The ability of a person to store, and recall information.
